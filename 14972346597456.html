title: English Plan 2014
author: imlarry
date: 2014-08-25

tags: English

I was very glad to have a class about learning how to learn. Learning
English is a big problem to me. When it came with words and phrases i
didn't know, I always gave up. I attempted many times. Now I think it is
a chance to change myself.

My Goal

The first thing I want to decide is that what level I want to master
English. My goal is that I can easily listen to TED Talks or VOA
English. And I will spent three months to finish it.

My tips

I myself want to give myself some tips to remainder myself.

  • Never Stop: Don't stop to do other things. If you stopped, you would lose your chance to put your chunks together and should return a lot back after several days. You will lose your chance to win.
  • Practice everyday: I think it is the most tough task for me. But I really need it if I want to master a language. Learning day by day is the best choice for building my solid wall of knowledge.
  • Focus on your process: Focusing on your process will give yourself some relief while you would be distracted if you focus on product.

The three tips above is very important for me to learning English well.

Week journal

I will put my journal of learning english here once a week. It can help
me see clear where am I, and the shortcoming of my plan.

another is the status of implementation.
