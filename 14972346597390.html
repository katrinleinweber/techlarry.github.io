title: My thinking about process vs. product
author: imlarry
date: 2014-08-25

tags: Psychology

My Story

One day afternoon, I came to the library. I hoped that I can finished a
chapter in the book in this afternoon. I was excited to have an
expactation that I would have finished a chapter after this afternoon. I
sat down and relaxed. I opened the book named digital signal processing.
I started reading. one page.....two pages.... three pages...... Oh! This
exercise was a bit diffcult for me. I should jump to next page, because
time was limited. Oh! This equation was so complex for me. I should jump
to next page. After a half of hour, I have no patience to read any
sentences. I said to myself, " You just need to relax, go home and a cup
of tea". Finally I actually returned home and didn't want to touch my
books any more.

Aanlysis

Did it happen accidentally? No! I tell you it happened many times in my
last year. And I should have no way to solve it. Now I find a good way
to solve it. The reason why i gave up reading is that I focused on
product in order to quickly finish my assignment. I became nervous and
nervous after I jumped what was difficult for me. Finally, I had no
patience. Every once I gave up some small difficult problems, I will
become a bit more upset.

Tips

Please focusing on your process. Learning a bit by a bit everyday is the
best learning method.
