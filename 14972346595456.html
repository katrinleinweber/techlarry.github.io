title: Oil Prospecting
author: imlarry
date: 2015-05-06
tags: Geophysics

slug: oil_prospecting

Oil prospecting is the most important procedure for oil companies. Other
relevant procedures are production, transportation and end-user sales.
For companies and investors, oil prospecting is a high-tech, high-risk
and high-reward activity.

Nowadays, oil companies widely use the technology of geology, geophysics
and scientific computing to improve the ability to find and locate the
oil reservoirs. Firstly, geologists should make a basic survey of a
targeted exploration area to provide the fundamental geology and
topography data. After the geophysical survey and data processing using
GPU parallel computing, the geophysicists can finally give the best
locations to well drilling.

It is common for oil companies to find out that wells haven’t any oil
after several million dollars investments. Although many high-tech
methods were being used in prospecting, the locations given by
geophysicists can totally deviate from its ideal targets. The reason is
that non-uniqueness, uncertainty, and complexity widely exists in
geologic models, which leads to the high-risk property of oil
prospecting. And this is also the reason why so many engineers and
scientists are dedicated to develop new high-tech method.

Big oil companies in the world all have earnings of more than 10 billion
dollars per year. You can easily find these big oil companies in the
Forbes list of the companies. National oil companies owned by government
can easily make money because of the absolute monopoly in a country.
Besides, the company whose country belongs to OPEC members usually have
very high net profit margin of 80% with the help of OPEC.

The most three distinct characteristics for oil prospecting are
high-tech, high-risk and high-reward. It is not surprising that all
high-tech companies have these characteristics, e.g., drug companies,
aircraft manufacturing companies. In my opinion, the property of
high-tech and high-risk will become more apparent, with the increase
difficulty of oil prospecting and large decrease of oil storage. And oil
companies may not earn tens of billions of dollars any more. Because new
energy companies nowadays act to promote the rapid widespread deployment
of advanced, clean, and sustainable energy sources.
